**Conservation of Momentum**
When one ball strikes the others, momentum is transferred through the line of balls, causing the ball on the opposite end to swing out.

**Conservation of Energy**
The total mechanical energy (ignoring losses like air resistance and friction) is approximately conserved, which is visible in the nearly symmetric motion of the balls.
